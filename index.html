About Bright Horizon Academy

Year of establishment: January 1st, 2020

The Proprietor/Chairman of Bright Horizon Academy, a trained, qualified teacher by profession has great passion to educate, guide and instill discipline in learners. This reason led him to establish the school on 1st March, 2021. with a population of five (5) pupils, two (2) teaching staff, five (5) non – teaching staff and Mrs. M.A. Peter as the head teacher. Presently, the school has fifteen (15) pupils, eleven (11) teaching staff, five (5) non – teaching staff and Mrs. M.A. Peter is still the head teacher.

We run Nigerian and British montesory curriculum. Our Teachers are well trained and retrain.
